Browse Profile

Simply browse our extensive database of adoptable animals, filtering by gender, breed, age,etc . Each pet's profile includes detailed information and adorable photos to help you get to know them better. Once you find a furry friend who catches your eye, simply click the "Adopt" button to initiate the next step.

Contact

To ensure a perfect match, after clicking "Interested," you'll be asked to complete a brief questionnaire about your lifestyle and preferences. Our adoption specialists will then review your application and the pet's profile to see if they're a good fit. If so, they'll contact you to schedule a meet-and-greet and answer any questions you have throughout the process.

About Us

We are Ready to Help You Find Your Perfect Companion

At Wags And Whiskers, we're driven by a love for animals and a belief that every creature deserves a safe and loving home. We're more than just an adoption platform; we're a passionate community dedicated to creating lasting connections between pets and their perfect families. Our user-friendly website makes finding your furry (or feathered, or scaled!) friend a breeze, with in-depth profiles and a supportive adoption process tailored to ensure a successful match.
